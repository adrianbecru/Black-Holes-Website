MISSION

Black holes are regions of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing can escape, not even light. Below are some other terms you might not be familiar with, hover over them!

Event Horizon

The boundary beyond which no information can escape.

Singularity

A point of infinite density where physics breaks down.

Time Dilation

Time slows dramatically near intense gravitational fields.

Beyond this point, time has no meaning.
